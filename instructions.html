INSTRUCTIONS

  1. The player will be subjected to many obstacles with different number of colors and rotating in different directions
  2. Difficulty levels 1-5 are based on speed of rotation of obstacles
  3. Do not resize screen while playing. This game is suitable to play in mobile as well
  4. Collecting 2 consecutive coins will slow down the rotation of next few obstacles that you cross within a specified time
  5. Tap to restart option allows you restart with same settings
  6. Stars are like checkpoints for the distance covered which help you increase score.
  7. White outlined blue stars are equivalent to travelling 10 checkpoints and morever they give you a power up ball that can pass through all obstacles
  8. But beware the power up ball has a life span of only 10 seconds
  9. Colliding with wrong color or falling down results in 'GAME OVER'
  10. Try to make it to the top 10 in the leaderboard. All the best!
  11. Press 'P' to pause the game while playing
  12. In multiplayermode the player who has high score wins, but a player can continue playing to achieve a high score even if other player has lost
  13. Use 'u' and 'up arrow' to play in multiplayer mode
